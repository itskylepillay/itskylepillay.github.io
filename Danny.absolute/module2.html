Module 2: Getting Started With No Experience

Objectives:

• Prepare your look and health.

• Learn the basics of posing, walking, and expressing.

Topics:

1. Self-Care:

Skin: Clean, moisturized, minimal makeup at castings.

Hair: Healthy & natural.

Nails: Clean & simple.

Fitness: Stay fit but healthy — no extreme diets.

2. Posing:

• Learn your angles in front of a mirror or camera.

• Avoid stiff poses — flow naturally.

• Watch campaigns & mimic professional models’ flow.

3. Facial Expressions:

• Neutral, soft smile, big smile, fierce, playful.

• Practice emotions: joy, confidence, serenity.

4. Runway Walk:

• Straight line, confident, strong posture.

• Practice turns & stops.

• Keep arms natural & relaxed.

Assignment:

• Practice poses & expressions daily for 20–30 minutes.

• Record a video of your walk and critique it.
