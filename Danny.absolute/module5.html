Module 5: Professional Skills & Mindset

Objectives:

• Be professional on set.

• Develop confidence & handle rejection gracefully.

Topics:

1. On-Set Etiquette:

• Arrive early, polite & cooperative.

• Don’t touch equipment without asking.

• Don’t bring unnecessary guests.

2. Networking:

• Exchange contacts professionally.

• Follow up with thank-you notes.

3. Handling Rejection:

• It’s about the client’s vision, not personal.

• Learn from feedback & move on.

4. Mindset:

• Practice positive affirmations.

• Focus on what you can control.

• Surround yourself with supportive people.

Assignment:

• Journal your strengths & what makes you unique.

• Write 3 ways to stay professional under pressure.
