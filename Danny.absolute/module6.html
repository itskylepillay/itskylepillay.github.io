Module 6: Growing Your Career

Objectives:

• Keep improving & adapting.

• Build your brand & reach wider opportunities.

Topics:

1. Portfolio Updates:

• Add new shoots every 6–12 months.

• Remove outdated or weaker shots.

2. Social Media Presence:

• Showcase your work professionally.

• Use hashtags & tag brands/photographers.

3. Continued Learning:

• Take acting or runway classes.

• Stay updated on trends & industry news.

4. Exploring Niches:

• Test international markets if possible.

• Explore commercials, acting, presenting.

Assignment:

• Create an Instagram highlight with your best work.

• Research 1–2 workshops or classes to take.
