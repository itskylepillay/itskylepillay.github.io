Module 4: Finding Representation & Jobs

Objectives:

• Learn how to approach agencies & apply to jobs.

• Protect yourself from scams.

Topics:

1. Finding an Agency:

• Search “Top modeling agencies in [your city/country].”

• Look at their roster — do you see similar models?

• Avoid agencies that demand big upfront fees.

2. Applying:

• Submit digitals, basic stats, short professional email.

• Attend open calls if available.

3. Freelancing:

• Create a website or use Instagram portfolio.

• Apply to online casting calls & network for referrals.

4. Red Flags:

• Agencies asking big payments to sign.

• Promises of guaranteed fame.

• Uncomfortable or inappropriate requests.

Assignment:

• Make a list of 10 agencies & apply to at least 5.

• Write & practice a 2–3 sentence self-introduction.
